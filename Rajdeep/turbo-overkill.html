TURBO OVERKILL


Background and Origins:

  • Developed by Trigger Happy Interactive, currently in development.
  • Set in a retro-futuristic cyberpunk world filled with neon lights and over-the-top action.

Game Setting:

  • Features a fast-paced, adrenaline-fueled gameplay experience.
  • Embraces the aesthetics of classic action movies and 80s/90s video games.

Gameplay Mechanics:

  • Introduces innovative movement mechanics, including wall-running and super jumps.
  • Players wield a variety of overpowered weapons and gadgets to decimate enemies.
  • Engage in high-octane combat against hordes of enemies and epic boss battles.

Characters:

  • Play as Johnny Turbo, a cybernetically enhanced badass on a quest for revenge.
  • Encounter a colorful cast of eccentric characters, including allies and foes.
  • Unravel a story filled with humor, wit, and plenty of over-the-top action.

Recent Updates and News:

  • Early access release planned for later this year, with regular updates and new content.
  • Community feedback and suggestions are actively being incorporated into the development process.
  • Stay tuned for announcements regarding release dates and additional features.
