CLOUDPUNK


Background and Origins:

  • Developed and published by Ion Lands, released in 2020.
  • Set in the sprawling cyberpunk city of Nivalis, players assume the role of a delivery driver for the semi-legal delivery company Cloudpunk.

Game Setting:

  • Features a vibrant voxel art style that brings the futuristic city to life.
  • Explores themes of artificial intelligence, inequality, and urban decay.

Gameplay Mechanics:

  • Players navigate the city in their hover car, completing delivery missions and exploring the open-world environment.
  • Choices made during interactions with NPCs influence the storyline and lead to different outcomes.
  • Upgrade the hover car and unlock new abilities to improve efficiency and complete missions.

Characters:

  • Meet a diverse cast of characters, including androids, AI, and humans, each with their own stories and motivations.
  • Interact with factions such as the powerful corporation Curzona, the hacker group The Honk, and the android liberation movement.

Recent Updates and News:

  • Expansion DLCs have been released, introducing new areas to explore and missions to complete.
  • Community mods and custom content continue to expand the Cloudpunk experience.
  • Rumors of a sequel or continuation of the Cloudpunk story have surfaced, generating excitement among fans.
